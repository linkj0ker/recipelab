BEBINCA

Bebinca or bibik is a pudding like Goan dessert with layers of cake made with coconut milk, eggs, butter and flour.

Ingredients For Bebinca


  • 200 Gram Refined Flour
  • 10 Egg Yolks
  • 500 Gram Sugar
  • 200 ml Coconut Milk
  • 5 Gram Nutmeg Powder
  • 200 Gram Butter

How To Make Bebinca

Step 1: Mix the coconut milk, flour, sugar, nutmeg and the egg yolks and make a batter.
Step 2: Grease an oven proof dish with a spoonful of melted butter, pour 75 ml of batter into the dish and spread evenly.
Step 3: Bake till golden brown.
Step 4: Spread another spoonful of butter and pour another 75 ml of batter over it and spread evenly.
Step 5: Bake and repeat this until all the batter is used up.
Step 6: Turn out the bebinca onto a wire rack.
Step 7: Cool and cut into slices before serving.

GOAN BAATH

Baath or Batica is the traditional Goan Coconut Cake. This Cake is also called as Batk or Baathika. For Goan Christmas sweet recipes, this Batica is very commonly made at most houses.

Ingredients For BAATH


  • 250 gms Rawa
  • 2 Fresh Scraped Coconut
  • 200 gms Butter
  • 2 tsp Ghee
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 300 gms Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

How To Make BAATH

Step 1: Grind the grated coconut with eggs and butter to a fine paste.
Step 2: Now add rava,salt and sugar to the mixture with 1tps baking powder and mix it well.
Step 3: Keep this paste for over night fermentation.
Step 4: Now put the mixture in a baking tray and bake the mixture at 180°C for 30 minutes.
Step 5: Serve it with a hot cup of tea.

Goan Vonn

Vonn is a Goan sweet which is prepared at special occasion or festival's in Goa. It is a must in Goan weddings.

Ingredients For Vonn


  • 4 Large Grated Coconuts
  • 600 Gms Palm Jaggery
  • 450 Gms Channa Dal
  • 1½ Cups Rice
  • 15 Cardamom
  • Salt
  • Water

How To Make Vonn

Step 1: Wash the chana dal till the water runs clear and soak it in a large vessel for 3-4 hours.
Step 2: After 2 hours, add the chana dal along with water and 2 tspn salt, in a pressure cooker, close the pressure cooker and boil the chana dal giving it just one whistle on medium flame.
Step 3: Wash the rice until the water runs clear and soak it in a bowl with 1 cup water for 30 minutes.
Step 4: After 30 minutes, in a mixer grinder, add the soaked rice and 15 green cardamoms and make a smooth fine paste of this mixture.
Step 5: In a mixer grinder, Extract the juice from the grated coconuts, thrice and pour it into a large vessel.
Step 6: Now add the grated jaggery to the coconut juice and stir it well.
Step 7: Now place this vessel with the coconut juice and jaggery on medium flame.
Step 8: Keep stirring,once the coconut juice starts boiling, pour the rice paste little by little.
Step 9: Once the Rice paste is added in, keep stirring for at least 15 minutes.
Step 10: Check if you require more jaggery, you can add it at this stage. Let it boil for another 7 minutes.
Step 11: Your Vonn is ready, eat it while it is hot. Add dry fruits according to your taste.
