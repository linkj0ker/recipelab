SORPOTEL

This Portuguese inspired dish is cooked in almost every catholic household in Goa for Christmas. It is said that authentic Sorpotel is heated for about 20 minutes each day after the feast and tastes best on the 3rd or 4th day.

Ingredients For Sorpotel


  • 1/2 Kilograms Meat(Pork)
  • 4 Large Onions
  • 8-10 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Inch Ginger Piece
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 2 Inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper Corn
  • 2 tsp Cloves
  • 10 Garlic Cloves
  • 15 Dry Red Kashmiri Chillies
  • 2 Marble Sized Tamarind
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • 1 tsp Sugar

How To Make Sorpotel

Step 1: Grind the Sorpotel masala and keep aside. Then boil the meat with sufficient water on medium-low heat with 2 tbsp salt and 1 tbsp Vinegar for about 25-30 min. Drain the meat and keep aside the drained water.
Step 2: Cut the meat into small cubes/pieces and shallow fry the cubes. Keep the fried meat aside for the next step.
Step 3: In a vessel, add a little oil in it and fry all the onions till they soften. Add red chilies, ginger and garlic to the soften onions. Fry it for a few minutes.
Step 4: Add the sorpotel masala and fry for about 2-3 minutes and then add the fried meat cubes.
Step 5: Add the drained meat water that was kept aside at Step :2 into the vessel along with sugar(according to your taste). Mix it well.
Step 6: Cook the meat on medium low heat for about 45 minutes till gravy thickens. And then cook for another 5 minutes.
Step 7: Serve hot Sorpotel with Sannas to enjoy its taste.

Rava Fried Fish

Anyone who has been to Goa or heard of Goan food will know this dish. Fish fillets flavoured with ginger, garlic and red chillies are fried to perfection and garnished with aromatic coriander.

Ingredients For Rava Fried Fish


  • 50 gms Rava
  • 3 Basa Fish
  • 2 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 50 ml Refined Oil
  • 2 Lemons
  • Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder

How To Make Rava Fried Fish

Step 1: Take some ginger garlic paste in a bowl and add chilli powder to it with some turmeric and salt.
Step 2: Add 2 spoons of refined oil and mix it well.
Step 3: Marinate the fish with the paste and then coat the pieces with rava.
Step 4: Add fair amount of refined oil in a preheat pan and shallow fry the fish.
Step 5: Lay some chopped coriander in a platter.
Step 6: Serve the fish into the platter with lemon pieces.

Fish Reacheado

Fish recheado is a Goan dish but the word recheado means stuffed in Portuguese. Here is a masala stuffed pomfret recipe by Chef Sainora that will surely make your mouth water.

Ingredients For Fish Reacheado


  • 1 Pomfret
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 Chopped Onion Large Size
  • 20 red chillies
  • 7 Cloves
  • 2 Cinnamon
  • 5 Green Cardamoms
  • 2 Pieces Of Ginger
  • 1 Pod Of Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Oil

How To Make Fish Reacheado

Step 1: Soak red chillies, cloves, cinnamon, green cardamoms, cumin seeds, black pepper corns, ginger and garlic in toddy vinegar for 12 hours. Then grind to a paste.
Step 2: Saute onions in 1 tsp oil on low flame till brown.
Step 3: Add sugar. Now add the sauteed onions to the prepared paste. The recheado masala is now ready.
Step 4: Clean and cut fish, then stuff it with reacheado paste.
Step 5: Add salt and lime juice.
Step 6: Refrigerate below 5 degrees centigrade 1 hour.
Step 7: Then shallow fry fish in 1 tsp oil for 7-8 minutes each side.
Step 8: Fish recheado is ready. It's best served hot with goan bread.
