Goan Canapiés

If you have ever been to a party in a Goa you might have spotted these little things.These canapes also known as Forminhas in Goa are served in goa as a snack in parties, etc.

Ingredients For Canapés


  • 1 Large Potato
  • 3-4 French Beans
  • 1 Carrot
  • 3 thsp Green Peas
  • Salt
  • Pepper Powder
  • Mayonnaise

How To Make Goan Canapés

Step 1: Peel and vut the potato and carrot into small cubes.
Step 2: In a vessel add 2 cup's of water and heat it for 2 minutes.Ane add a pinch of salt to it and let it boil.
Step 3: Now add the chopped potato into the water and let it boil for 2 minutes. Then add the chopped carrot into the vessel and let it cook with the potatoes for 1 minute.
Step 4: Now add the green peas and the french beans into the vessel and let it cook for 2 minutes with the potatoes and carrots.
Step 5: After cooking the veggies drai it.
Step 6: Put the drained veggies in a bowl and add salt, mayonnaise and pepper powder to the bowl and mix it well.
Step 7: Scoop some mixture in a canopie and serve it.

Choris Pao

Chaoris Pao is considered as a street food in Goa but it is also served as a snack in small eateries and restaurants. It can be enjoyed at breakfast, as a snack or a small meal.

Ingredients For Choris Pao


  • Goan Chorisa/Goan Sausage
  • 4 Large Sliced Onions
  • 1 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 2 tbsps Goan Vinegar
  • 1 cup Water
  • Salt

How To Make Choris Pao

Step 1: Seperate the sausage from its casing.
Step 2: Take a vessel and add oil to it, after hearing reduce the flame and add the sliced onions to it and wait till it softens.
Step 3: Now add the chorisa into the vessel and heat till it softens and releases fat.
Step 4: Add some water and let the chorisa cook for some time.
Step 5: Add vinegar and adjust the seasoning as per your taste.
Step 6: Add some sauce and serve it in a pao
Step 7: Bakery pao are preferred to rnjoy it's Goan taste.

Fish Croquettes

Ingredients For Fish Crowuettes


  • 1 Chopped Onion Medium Size
  • 4 Chopped Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Chopped Green Chilies
  • Black Pepper Powder
  • 2 Can's Of Tuna
  • Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Goan Vinegar
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 2 Smashed Potatoes Small Size
  • 2 tbps Chopped Coriander
  • 1 Egg

How To Make Rava Fish Croquettes

Step 1: In a vessel, add 3 tbsp oil and fry onions till it softens. Add the chopped garlic and green chilies to it. Heat it for 2 minutes.
Step 2: Add the canned tuna fish along with pepper powder, turmeric, vinegar, salt into the vessel and mix it well. Cook for a few minutes till mixture is almost dry.
Step 3: Add the boiled mashed potatoes , chopped coriander and mix it well.
Step 4: Now remove the mixture and shape into croquettes.
Step 5: Now take an egg in a bowl and beat it. Add some water and make the egg wash. Now dip the croquettes in egg wash and then into bread crumbs.
Step 6: Deep fry them till golden brown.
Step 7: Serve with sauces of your liking.
